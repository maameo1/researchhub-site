Features

Everything your lit review needs.
Nothing it doesn't.

ResearchHub turns your paper collection into an intelligent, interconnected research knowledge base.

AI Summaries

30-second paper intelligence

Every paper gets a TLDR, key contributions, methods, limitations, open questions, relevance to your research, and real citations to follow — all generated instantly.

Relationship Graph

See how papers connect

Interactive, zoomable graph showing which papers share methods, authors, tags, and keywords. Auto-detected clusters with bridge papers that connect different research threads.

Gap Analysis

Find what's missing

AI identifies themes, contradictions, missing baselines, and unexplored directions across your entire library. Get search queries to fill the gaps before reviewers find them.

Weekly Reading

Your personal reading advisor

AI recommends which papers to read next based on what you've recently read, with a coherent theme and reading order. Suggests new papers you haven't discovered yet.

Method Schematics

Visualize any pipeline

Generate clean, publication-style SVG diagrams of any paper's methodology. Color-coded stages from input to output, ready for your presentations.

Import Anywhere

Bring your whole library

Zotero sync, arXiv URL, DOI, title search, PDF upload, BibTeX import, CSV import. Your papers are in ResearchHub in minutes, not hours.
